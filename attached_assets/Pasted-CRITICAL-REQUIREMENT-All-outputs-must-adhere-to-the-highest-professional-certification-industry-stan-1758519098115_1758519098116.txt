CRITICAL REQUIREMENT
All outputs must adhere to the highest professional certification industry standards. These are not informal documents; they are business-critical reports that directly inform and validate significant investment decisions, audit outcomes, and certification strategies for our clients.

DOCUMENTATION SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
This system is designed to transform raw assessment data into a suite of professional documents tailored for specific audiences. The architecture is built around the following four distinct document types and their corresponding templates.

Document Types and Purposes:

PDF Technical Report (Template: pdf_temp_export.pdf)

This is a comprehensive, audit-ready technical document intended for certification bodies and internal implementation teams. It provides an in-depth record of the assessment findings, ensuring full transparency and compliance.

Excel Management Dashboard (Template: excel_temp_export.pdf)

An interactive workbook for internal project managers and operational teams. Its purpose is to facilitate the tracking of project progress, monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), and manage ongoing compliance efforts.

Word Executive Summary (Template: word_temp_export.pdf)

A concise, high-level business report created for executives and key stakeholders. It translates complex technical information into clear, plain-language insights to support strategic decision-making and provide a clear overview of the assessment results.

Email Consultation Request (Template: email_temp_export.pdf)

A professional business communication used for business development and client outreach. It formally presents the assessment findings to the client, initiating the consultation process to discuss next steps toward R2v3 certification.

DATA INTEGRATION SPECIFICATIONS
The system operates by integrating and transforming raw data from a centralized source into the required output formats.

Input Source:

The web application system receives and processes comprehensive R2v3 assessment data, including:

Company Information: Details such as company name, contact information, physical address, and industry classification.

Assessment Metrics: Detailed scoring, including the overall percentage, specific category breakdowns, and a comprehensive gap analysis.

Gap Analysis: Identification of critical, major, and minor non-conformities with detailed descriptions.

R2v3 Requirement Mappings: Precise citations of the specific R2v3 standard requirements.

Implementation Planning: Timelines and cost estimates for addressing identified gaps and achieving compliance.

Scope Definitions: Clarification of the R2v3 scope, including the specific REC categories, appendices, and operational activities.

Output Generation:

The system must transform this raw assessment data into the four distinct professional documents mentioned above, leveraging the provided templates to ensure consistency and a professional presentation.
