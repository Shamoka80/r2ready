Below is a **deployment-ready curriculum plan** for both programs. Each module includes learning objectives, estimated duration, assessment type, and training delivery format suitable for in-person or LMS integration.

-----
**A. R2v3 Business Implementation Training Program**

**Audience:** Recycling/reuse facility managers, compliance officers, EHS coordinators, and quality leads.\
**Goal:** Equip participants to implement all R2v3 (v3.1) requirements, prepare for certification, and maintain compliance.\
**Total Duration:** ~28 hours (5 days or modular self-paced).\
**Certification:** *Certificate of Completion – R2v3 Business Implementation.*

-----

|**Module**|**Title**|**Learning Objectives**|**Duration**|**Assessment**|**Delivery**|
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
|1|R2v3 Overview & Certification Fundamentals|Understand R2v3 purpose, certification structure, updates in v3.1, roles (facility, CB, SERI).|2h|Short quiz + discussion|Lecture + slides|
|2|Core 1 – Scope Definition|Define certification scope, identify facilities and controlled processes, prepare scope statement.|1\.5h|Scope mapping exercise|Workshop|
|3|Core 2 – Responsible Management Hierarchy|Apply reuse-first hierarchy, develop management policy and process flow.|1\.5h|Policy writing assignment|Case study|
|4|Core 3 – EHS Management System|Integrate ISO 14001/45001 or RIOS into R2 compliance; create EHSMS documentation.|2h|Documentation checklist|Workshop + template|
|5|Core 4 – Legal and Other Requirements|Build legal compliance plan, understand import/export and labor laws, maintain audit records.|2h|Quiz + plan draft|Interactive lecture|
|6|Core 5 – Tracking Throughput|Learn recordkeeping standards, inventory limits, data logging templates.|1\.5h|Practical log exercise|Hands-on demo|
|7|Core 6 – Sorting, Categorization & Processing|Use REC categories, manage Controlled Streams, identify reuse vs. recovery routes.|2h|REC classification exercise|Simulation|
|8|Core 7 – Data Security|Develop Data Sanitization Plan, define secured areas, and handle incident response.|2h|Plan draft + audit roleplay|Workshop|
|9|Core 8 – Focus Materials|Build FM Management Plan, map downstream vendors, integrate into EHSMS.|1\.5h|FM flowchart creation|Practical session|
|10|Core 9–10 – Facility & Transport|Establish facility standards, insurance, closure plan, and secure transport methods.|1\.5h|Checklist review|Guided practice|
|11–17|Appendices A–G|Apply appendix requirements relevant to facility scope (A–G). Develop applicable documentation.|6h (45–60min each)|Appendix worksheets|Modular self-paced|
|18|Internal Auditing & Gap Closure|Conduct internal audits, classify findings, draft corrective actions.|2h|Mock audit|Role-play audit|
|19|Continuous Improvement & Surveillance|Plan annual surveillance, update documentation, maintain readiness.|1h|Review activity|Lecture|
|20|Digital Tools & Reporting|Use readiness and reporting tools (Excel dashboard, PDF/Word templates).|1h|Data entry exercise|Demo|

**Capstone Assessment:**\
Final facility simulation: participants complete a mock intake, REC mapping, and readiness report (graded 70%+ to pass).

-----
**B. R2v3 Auditor / Certification Body Training Program**

**Audience:** Certification body auditors, lead assessors, consultants, and technical experts.\
**Goal:** Ensure consistent, competent auditing to R2v3 (v3.1) and SERI Code of Practices.\
**Total Duration:** ~24 hours (4 days or modular online).\
**Certification:** *Certificate of Competency – R2v3 Auditor/Agency.*

-----

|**Module**|**Title**|**Learning Objectives**|**Duration**|**Assessment**|**Delivery**|
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
|1|SERI & R2v3 Governance Framework|Explain SERI’s structure, Code of Practices, and accreditation process.|1\.5h|Quiz|Lecture|
|2|Certification Process Lifecycle|Understand Stage 1 & 2 audits, scope verification, and certification cycles.|2h|Case evaluation|Workshop|
|3|Core Requirements Audit (1–10)|Apply audit techniques and identify objective evidence for each Core Requirement.|4h|Evidence mapping exercise|Interactive session|
|4|Appendix Audit Deep Dive (A–G)|Determine appendix applicability, assess documentation, and record findings.|3h|Scenario-based quiz|Workshop|
|5|REC Mapping & Technical Evaluation|Audit REC consistency, verify controlled stream categorization and traceability.|2h|REC verification lab|Simulation|
|6|Legal, EHS, & Data Security Verification|Evaluate compliance registers, FM plans, and data security controls during audit.|2h|Case-based review|Lecture + demo|
|7|Nonconformance Classification & Reporting|Distinguish major vs. minor findings, root cause verification, and report writing.|2h|Report writing task|Practical|
|8|Auditor Ethics & Impartiality|Apply ISO 19011 and SERI impartiality standards; manage conflicts and confidentiality.|1h|Ethics quiz|Discussion|
|9|Mock Facility Audit Simulation|Perform full simulated audit, record evidence, classify nonconformities.|4h|Group audit report|Field simulation|
|10|Calibration & Continuous Learning|Maintain competency; interpret SERI Formal Interpretations and updates.|1\.5h|Peer calibration review|Workshop|

**Capstone Assessment:**\
Participants perform a simulated R2v3 Stage 2 audit on a model facility case study, submit report, and defend findings in oral review (pass mark ≥75%).

-----
**Implementation Resources for Both Programs**

- Trainer guide and PowerPoint deck per module.
- R2v3 Compliance Checklist (auditor and business versions).
- Sample documentation templates: EHSMS, FM Plan, Data Sanitization Plan, Closure Plan.
- Case study packets (mock facilities with sample data).
- Assessment rubrics and LMS quiz banks.
