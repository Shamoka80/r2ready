# R2v3 Compliance Templates – Group 1

This Markdown file contains three editable templates for R2v3 documentation:  
**EHSMS Manual**, **Legal Compliance Register**, and **Data Sanitization Plan.**  
Each section provides structured placeholders and sample content to align with R2v3 Core Requirements.

---

## 1. R2v3 Environmental, Health & Safety Management System (EHSMS) Manual Template

**Document Title:** Environmental, Health & Safety Management System Manual  
**Standard Reference:** R2v3 Core Requirement 3; ISO 14001/45001; RIOS  

### 1.1 Purpose
This manual defines how the organization manages environmental, health, and safety (EHS) aspects and ensures full integration with R2v3 requirements.

### 1.2 Scope
- Applies to all facility operations related to the collection, reuse, repair, dismantling, and recycling of electronic equipment.  
- Includes all controlled processes, materials, and downstream partners.

### 1.3 Policy Statement
The organization commits to:
- Compliance with all legal, regulatory, and R2v3 requirements.
- Prevention of pollution, injury, and ill health.
- Continuous improvement of EHS performance.

### 1.4 Organizational Roles
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| EHS Manager | Maintain EHSMS documentation, audits, and reporting |
| Operations Lead | Implement and enforce safety procedures |
| All Employees | Follow EHS instructions and report unsafe acts |

### 1.5 Key Elements
- Risk Assessment and Control Procedures  
- Training and Competency Programs  
- Incident Reporting and Corrective Action  
- Internal Audit and Management Review  
- Integration with Focus Materials Management Plan and Data Security Policy  

### 1.6 Document Control
All revisions shall be recorded in the revision history below.

| Version | Date | Description | Approved By |
|----------|------|--------------|--------------|
| 1.0 | YYYY-MM-DD | Initial issue | EHS Manager |

## 2. R2v3 Legal Compliance Register Template

**Document Title:** Legal Compliance Register  
**Standard Reference:** R2v3 Core Requirement 4  

### 2.1 Purpose
To identify, maintain, and demonstrate compliance with all applicable environmental, health, safety, and data security laws.

### 2.2 Structure
| Regulation / Requirement | Applicable Jurisdiction | Description | Responsible Department | Frequency of Review | Compliance Status | Evidence Reference |
|---------------------------|-------------------------|--------------|-------------------------|---------------------|-------------------|--------------------|
| Example: EPA Hazardous Waste Permit | Federal | Regulates storage of hazardous e-waste | Compliance | Annual | ✅ Compliant | Permit No. #### |
| Example: Data Protection Law | State | Governs handling of customer data | IT Security | Annual | ⚠ Partial | Policy DS-01 |

### 2.3 Review Procedure
- Compliance Officer updates register quarterly.  
- Legal changes reviewed annually or as required.  
- Any non-compliance triggers a Corrective Action Report.

### 2.4 Records Retention
Maintain all permits, licenses, and proof of submission for **minimum 3 years**.

## 3. R2v3 Data Sanitization Plan Template

**Document Title:** Data Sanitization Plan  
**Standard Reference:** R2v3 Core Requirement 7; Appendix B; NIST 800-88  

### 3.1 Purpose
To ensure all data-bearing devices are securely sanitized or destroyed before reuse or recycling.

### 3.2 Scope
Applies to all incoming, stored, and processed data-bearing equipment including hard drives, SSDs, mobile devices, and network equipment.

### 3.3 Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Data Protection Representative | Oversee data sanitization process and verification |
| Technicians | Execute approved sanitization methods |
| Quality Assurance | Validate and record completion logs |

### 3.4 Sanitization Methods
| Device Type | Method | Verification Process | Reference Standard |
|--------------|---------|---------------------|--------------------|
| HDD | Logical overwrite (3-pass) | 5% sampling verified via recovery software | NIST 800-88 |
| SSD | Physical shredding | 100% verification by visual inspection | NSA EPL listed shredder |

### 3.5 Data Security Controls
- Restricted access to data processing area.  
- CCTV recording (minimum 60 days retention).  
- Dual-authorization for device release post-sanitization.  

### 3.6 Documentation and Records
| Record Type | Retention | Responsible Person |
|--------------|------------|--------------------|
| Sanitization Logs | 3 years | Data Protection Rep |
| Verification Reports | 3 years | QA |
| Incident Reports | 5 years | Compliance Officer |

### 3.7 Incident Response
Any suspected data breach must be reported within 24 hours. Investigation records to be attached to the **Incident Response Log**.
