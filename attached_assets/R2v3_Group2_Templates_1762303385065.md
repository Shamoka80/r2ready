# R2v3 Compliance Templates – Group 2

This Markdown file contains three editable templates for R2v3 documentation:  
**Focus Materials Management Plan**, **Internal Audit Checklist**, and **Training Matrix.**  
Each section aligns with R2v3 Core Requirements 3, 8, and the appendices on Focus Materials and audit management.

---

## 1. R2v3 Focus Materials Management Plan (FMMP) Template

**Document Title:** Focus Materials Management Plan  
**Standard Reference:** R2v3 Core Requirement 8; Appendix E; Appendix G  

### 1.1 Purpose
To define the procedures for safe handling, storage, and downstream management of all Focus Materials (FMs), ensuring environmental protection and regulatory compliance.

### 1.2 Scope
Applies to all operations involving Focus Materials including mercury, CRT glass, batteries, circuit boards, PCBs, and photovoltaic modules.

### 1.3 Identification of Focus Materials
| Focus Material | Source/Equipment | Quantity Estimate | Handling Procedure | Downstream Vendor |
|----------------|------------------|-------------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| CRT Glass | Displays, monitors | 200 kg/month | Stored in sealed drums | R2 Certified Recycler |
| Mercury Lamps | Lighting | 20 pcs/month | Double-bagged, labeled as hazardous | Mercury Recovery Facility |
| Batteries | Various | 50 kg/month | Sorted by chemistry, stored in fireproof bins | Battery Recycler |

### 1.4 Controls and Storage
- FMs stored in clearly labeled, weather-protected, and segregated areas.  
- PPE required for all handlers (gloves, masks, goggles).  
- Secondary containment and spill kits maintained nearby.

### 1.5 Downstream Management
- Only qualified R2-certified vendors approved.  
- Due diligence records retained for each downstream vendor.  
- Vendor requalification occurs annually or upon incident.

### 1.6 Documentation and Records
| Record Type | Responsible Party | Retention Period |
|--------------|-------------------|------------------|
| FM Tracking Logs | EHS Officer | 3 years |
| Vendor Qualifications | Compliance Manager | 3 years |
| Shipment Receipts | Logistics Lead | 3 years |

## 2. R2v3 Internal Audit Checklist Template

**Document Title:** Internal Audit Checklist  
**Standard Reference:** R2v3 Core Requirements 1–10; Appendices A–G  

### 2.1 Purpose
To verify compliance with all R2v3 requirements through structured internal audits, ensuring readiness for certification audits.

### 2.2 Structure
| Requirement | Audit Question | Evidence | Compliance (Y/N) | Comments / Actions |
|--------------|----------------|-----------|------------------|--------------------|
| Core 1 – Scope | Is the R2 certificate scope accurate and up to date? | Certificate, scope statement |  |  |
| Core 3 – EHSMS | Is an EHSMS implemented and maintained per ISO 14001/45001? | EHSMS manual, audit report |  |  |
| Core 7 – Data Security | Are data sanitization records and verification logs available? | Logs, policy |  |  |
| Core 8 – Focus Materials | Are FM flowcharts and downstream vendor qualifications current? | FMMP, records |  |  |
| Appendix B – Data Sanitization | Are verification samples and destruction logs compliant with NIST 800-88? | Test reports |  |  |

### 2.3 Audit Process
- Auditor conducts interviews, reviews records, and observes operations.  
- Findings classified as **Conformity**, **Minor NC**, or **Major NC**.  
- Corrective Actions recorded in the Corrective Action Log.

### 2.4 Reporting and Review
Audit results are presented to top management during the annual Management Review meeting. Reports retained for a minimum of **3 years**.

**Document Title:** Training and Competency Matrix  
**Standard Reference:** R2v3 Core Requirement 3.7; Core Requirement 10; Appendix B  

### 3.1 Purpose
To record, monitor, and maintain staff competency and training relevant to their assigned roles within the R2v3 management system.

### 3.2 Structure

### 3.3 Competency Evaluation
- Post-training assessments required for all technical and safety modules.  
- Evaluations scored ≥80% considered competent.  
- Retraining triggered upon changes to R2v3 requirements or job reassignment.

### 3.4 Records Retention
All training records retained for **minimum 3 years** and made available during certification audits.
