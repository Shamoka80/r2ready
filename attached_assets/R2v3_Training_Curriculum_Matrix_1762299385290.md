# **R2v3 Comprehensive Training Curriculum Matrix**
## **A. Business Implementation Training Program**

|Module|Title|Learning Objectives|Duration|Assessment Type|Delivery Mode|
| :- | :- | :- | :- | :- | :- |
|1|R2v3 Overview & Fundamentals|Understand R2v3 purpose, structure, updates, roles|2h|Quiz + discussion|Lecture|
|2|Core 1: Scope Definition|Define certification scope and controlled processes|1\.5h|Scope mapping exercise|Workshop|
|3|Core 2: Responsible Management|Apply reuse-first hierarchy, develop management policy|1\.5h|Policy writing assignment|Case Study|
|4|Core 3: EHS Management System|Integrate ISO/RIOS into R2, create EHSMS documentation|2h|Documentation checklist|Workshop|
|5|Core 4: Legal & Other Requirements|Build compliance plan, import/export laws, fair labor|2h|Plan draft|Interactive Lecture|
|6|Core 5: Tracking Throughput|Recordkeeping, inventory, legal limits, digital logs|1\.5h|Practical log exercise|Demo|
|7|Core 6: Categorization & Processing|Use REC, classify materials, manage streams|2h|Classification exercise|Simulation|
|8|Core 7: Data Security|Create sanitization plan, secured areas, breach response|2h|Plan draft + roleplay|Workshop|
|9|Core 8: Focus Materials|Develop FM Management Plan and flowchart|1\.5h|FM flowchart creation|Practice Session|
|10|Core 9-10: Facility & Transport|Facility standards, closure plan, secure transport|1\.5h|Checklist review|Guided Practice|
|11–17|Appendices A–G|Apply appendix requirements and develop documentation|6h|Appendix worksheets|Modular Self-Paced|
|18|Internal Audit & Gap Closure|Conduct mock audits and corrective actions|2h|Mock audit|Role-play|
|19|Continuous Improvement|Maintain readiness, manage surveillance|1h|Review activity|Lecture|
|20|Digital Tools & Reporting|Use readiness tools and reporting templates|1h|Data entry exercise|Demo|


## **B. Auditor / Certification Body Training Program**

|Module|Title|Learning Objectives|Duration|Assessment Type|Delivery Mode|
| :- | :- | :- | :- | :- | :- |
|1|SERI & R2v3 Governance|Explain SERI structure, Code of Practices, accreditation|1\.5h|Quiz|Lecture|
|2|Certification Lifecycle|Understand audit stages, scope verification, cycles|2h|Case evaluation|Workshop|
|3|Core Req. Audit (1–10)|Identify evidence and verify compliance|4h|Evidence mapping|Interactive|
|4|Appendix Audit (A–G)|Assess appendix applicability and findings|3h|Scenario quiz|Workshop|
|5|REC Mapping Evaluation|Verify categorization and traceability|2h|Verification lab|Simulation|
|6|Legal & Data Security|Audit compliance plans, FM plans, and controls|2h|Case review|Lecture + demo|
|7|Nonconformance & Reporting|Classify major/minor findings, write reports|2h|Report task|Practical|
|8|Ethics & Impartiality|Apply ISO 19011 and SERI ethics standards|1h|Quiz|Discussion|
|9|Mock Audit Simulation|Perform simulated facility audit and reporting|4h|Group audit|Field Simulation|
|10|Calibration & Continuous Learning|Maintain competency and interpret updates|1\.5h|Peer review|Workshop|
